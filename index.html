Tim Berners-Lee

The founder of WWW:World Wide Web

Hi!

Tim Berners-Lee is a software engineer who invented the Internet (or the World Wide Web) in 1989 while at CERN,the EUROPIAN PARTICLE PHYSICS LABORATORY. He is director of the World Wide Web consortium that he founded & he is a professor in Computer Science Deparment in University Of Oxford. In 2001 he became a Fellow of the Royal Socitey & in 2004 he was Knighted.

In 1994 Berners-lee founded World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) at the laboratory of Computer Science (LCS) at the Massachusetts Institute of Tecnology in Boston.It was made up of companies that wanted to work together to create web & patents. Berners-Lee has served as a director of this W3C since it was founded. Microsoft produced the first version of Internet Explorer & it's commercial browser created enormous growth in websites & web users. The Web was first used for e-commerce in 1997.
